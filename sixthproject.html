Freelance mission: Website development & Brand building for a restoration business


This work is dedicated for the owner of Venti CAFE-RESTAURANT in Khzema, Sousse (Tunisia) who sought the growth of his business. He was looking for an additional marketing channel and refining his market positioning.
The suggestion of a website for his brand contained the following aspects:

  • Enhancing Brand Identity
  • Presentation & Layout design
  • Understanding of the market positioning

Based on the Web Developement requirements and users' research we created a rough prototype and basic wireframes of the suggested media channel of promotion to our Client VENTI CAFE.
