STORYTELLING PROJECT: Minimalist design is a persuation strategy in desguise


"Ce qui manque aux orateurs en profondeur, ils vous le donnent en longueur" Montesquieu.
This project is about creating a persuasive content with fewer words. I conceived an immersive narrative that evolves through the intuitive scrolling and sliding of the menu rubrics. The purpose of this project is to formulate the customer's need to send an insightful apology to his client using the technique of storytelling. The anticipated outcome of this project is to help our customer draw refined promises for his client as a projection of commitment in the future. Further information about the theme of the project can not be displayed for privacy concerns. Only a prototype of the storytelling interface is displayed here: Link of the Storytelling Prototype.
Stories define us; Stories change the way we perceive the word, thus the way we act. But, How?

OUR STRATEGY OF CONTENT GENERATION

The strategy is mainly about creating interest. Curiosity has a very strong impact on our mind. Then, Sincerity has the similar impact on our emotions. Lastly, the Value highlighted through this project guarantees the durability of such impact. What remains is a call for action to activate that impact!
Of course, providing all the building blocks in a compelling and coherent visualization is necessary to turn a project from an idea to a concrete marketing strategy.
